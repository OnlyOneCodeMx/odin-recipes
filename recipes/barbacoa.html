Mexican Bef Barbeque

Description

This delicious recipe for Homemade Beef Barbecue is adapted to be very easy to cook. It will be very juicy and ideal for a taquiza.

Ingredientes

  • 1 head of garlic, for the marinade.
  • 1 teaspoon cumin, whole, for marinade.
  • 1 teaspoon oregano, for the marinade.
  • 1 teaspoon black pepper, for the marinade.
  • 2 bay leaves, for the marinade.
  • 8 guajillo chilies, for the marinade.
  • 3/4 cups beef broth, for the marinade.
  • 500 gr of beef rib.
  • 500 gr of beef chamberete.
  • 1kg of beef fillet.
  • enough roasted banana leaf.
  • enough avocado leaf.
  • 2 cups beef broth.
  • 1 cup diced carrots.
  • 1 cup chickpeas.
  • Enough epazote leaves.
  • 2 tomatoes for the red sauce.
  • 5 tree chilies for the red sauce.
  • 2 garlic for the red sauce.
  • 1/4 onion for the red sauce.
  • 1 teaspoon coriander seed for the red sauce.
  • Enough finely chopped onion.
  • Enough chopped coriander.
  • Enough lemon.
  • Enough tortilla.

Steps

  1. In a comal over low heat, roast the garlic, cumin, oregano, bay leaf, guajillo chilie and set aside.
  2. Blend the above with the beef broth, salt and pepper. Set aside.
  3. Place the chickpeas, carrots and epazote in the bottom of the steamer, pour the water, season with salt and pepper, place the steamer rack and set aside.
  4. In the steamer place the roasted plantain leaf to make a bed, add the beef rib, chamberete and beef fillet, place the avocado leaves, pour the marinade, cover with the plantain leaf and cook over medium heat for 2 hours.
  5. For the red sauce, in a comal over medium heat, roast the tomatoes, garlic, onion, chiles de arbol and set aside.
  6. Blend the sauce ingredients, cilantro seeds, season with salt and pepper. Serve on a plate, set aside.
