Black "Mole" with chocolate

Description

Delicious and easy recipe of mole poblano with rice Mexican style, this is one of the traditional dishes of Mexico.

Ingredients

  • 2 legs, and chicken thigh
  • 4 ancho chilies
  • 6 mulato chilies
  • 1 pasilla chilie
  • 1 chipotel chilie
  • 25 grams of raisins
  • 25 grams of almonds peeled
  • 25 grams of walnuts
  • 25 grams of almonds peeled
  • 15 grams of sesame seeds
  • 1 cinnamon stick
  • 1 clove
  • 1 coarse pepper
  • 2 garlic cloves, 1 for grinding and 1 for cooking with the chicken
  • 3 tomatoes
  • 1 onion, half a piece for the mole and half a piece to cook the chicken
  • Half tortilla
  • Half Boli
  • A quarter of French bread
  • A quarter of piloncillo
  • salt to taste
  • 2 cups chicken broth
  • Half a slab of table chocolate, preferably Mexican

Steps

  1. Cook the chicken with water, Â½ piece of onion and 1 clove of garlic for 30 minutes over medium heat. Set aside.
  2. Remove the seeds from the chiles and fry them with a little lard. Heat the water and when it boils add the chilies and cook until soft. Blend with a little water to form a paste. Set aside.
  3. Roast the tomatoes, onion and garlic. Set aside.
  4. In a separate pan, roast the walnuts, almonds, sesame seeds, prunes and raisins.
  5. Grill the tortilla and the bread, taking care not to burn them. Set aside.
  6. Blend everything you roasted previously with a little chicken broth or water until you obtain a very smooth paste. Set aside.
  7. Heat the lard in a clay pot over medium heat, add the chilies paste and fry for 5 minutes, stirring constantly.
  8. Add the nut mixture to the chilies, chocolate and heat over low heat, stirring constantly; gradually add chicken broth until desired consistency is reached and cook for 40 minutes to an hour and a half over medium heat, stirring constantly.
  9. Add the piloncillo with the chocolate, mix well and season.
  10. Coat the chicken with the mole and garnish with the sesame seeds.
  11. add your favorite rice and serve
