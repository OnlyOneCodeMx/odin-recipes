Red pork pozole

Description

Enjoy a traditional red pork pozole, which will become one of your favorite dishes. This preparation is made with a broth based on chili peppers, corn kernels, accompanied by shredded pork meat. To serve it, finely chopped lettuce, radish slices, oregano, onion and lemon are added. Red pork pozole is easier to prepare than you think; so don't hesitate to prepare it for the national holidays. A perfect dish for an important celebration or for the national holidays, as it has an incomparable flavor.

Ingredientes

  • 600 grams of precooked corn.
  • 3 peeled garlic cloves.
  • 1 onion, one quarter chopped for the corn, the rest finely chopped.
  • 3 tablespoons salt.
  • 1 kg of lean pork.
  • 3 tablespoons salt.
  • 5 deveined guajillo chiles.
  • 5 deveined ancho chilies.
  • 5 coarse peppers.
  • 5 radishes, wash, disinfect and dry. Cut into slices.
  • 1/4 cups romaine lettuce, wash, disinfect and dry. Finely chopped.
  • 10 lemons
  • 1/2 cups sour cream
  • 10 toasts

Steps

  1. In a medium saucepan, cook the corn along with the garlic, onion, meat and salt until the corn and meat are well cooked.
  2. In a small pot, soften the chiles in hot water for 5 minutes, and grind them in a blender with a little water. Add the paste to the corn and beef broth.
  3. Remove the meat, shred it finely.
  4. Check the corn for doneness and season.
  5. Serve with the meat and garnishes.
